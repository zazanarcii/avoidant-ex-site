The Avoidant Ex

Love-Bombed & Ghosted: How My Narcissistic Avoidant Ex Played Me Like a Fool.

The Story

I dated this girl for almost a year my first long-distance relationship. She lived in the UK, and I was new to the whole distance thing, but at first, it felt amazing...

The Slow Unraveling

The first few months were perfect... I was the only one keeping us alive.

  • She couldn't handle emotional intimacy
  • Any conflict made her shut down completely
  • Every serious conversation ended with her threatening to leave

The Cycle of Breakups

Seven breakups. Seven fucking times...

  • Researching how to love an avoidant partner
  • Going to therapy to "fix" myself
  • Swallowing every hurtful comment
  • Running every time things got real
  • Using me for attention when it suited her
  • Discarding me like trash the second she got bored

The Final Betrayal

  • Blocked me everywhere within minutes
  • Told everyone I was the toxic one
  • Was probably already lining up her next distraction

The Aftermath

  • ✅ Already moved on to someone new (probably)
  • ✅ Painting me as the villain to everyone
  • ✅ Never taking accountability for the damage she caused

What I Know Now

  • Love shouldn't leave you lonely
  • Their trauma isn't your responsibility
  • When someone shows you who they are, believe them

Now? I'm picking up the pieces...
